About This Portfolio

This portfolio serves as a comprehensive collection of my assignments completed as part of a Faculty Development Program (FDP). Each assignment is a hands-on exploration of a specific concept, demonstrating my practical understanding and application of key principles in the field.

The purpose of this collection is to showcase my learning journey and the tangible outcomes of my engagement with the course material. It includes projects, case studies, and reflections that highlight my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.

This website is structured to allow easy navigation through each assignment, providing a clear overview of the problem, the solution, and a personal reflection on the learning experience.
